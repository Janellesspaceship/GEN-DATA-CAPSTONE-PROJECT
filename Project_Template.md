
`1. What is the problem you are solving?`

# THE HIDDEN PATTERNS OF HOTEL BOOKINGS: Demand, Pricing & Cancellations

Hotels in Kenya struggle to understand booking patterns, cancellations, and pricing. This leads to lost revenue, empty rooms, and poor decision-making. The project solves this by analyzing hotel data to help hotels make better business decisions using data instead of guesswork.

**(A).What factors most influence hotel booking cancellations?**

Target column: is_canceled

Analysis:
- lead time vs cancellation
- deposit type vs cancellation
- repeated guests vs cancellation
- special requests vs cancellation

Insight idea:

- Longer lead times increase cancellation probability

**(B).Which hotel type (City vs Resort) has higher demand and revenue?**

Target Columns:

- hotel
- adr
- stays_in_week_nights + stays_in_weekend_nights

Insight idea:

- Which hotel gets more bookings
- Which generates higher revenue per booking

**(C).What are the peak booking months and seasons?**

Target Columns:

- arrival_date_month
- arrival_date_year
- hotel

Insight idea:

“August and December have the highest booking demand.”

visualizations:

- monthly booking trends
- seasonality patterns

**(D).What customer segments bring the most valuable bookings?**

Target Columns:

- customer_type
- adr
- market_segment
- special_requests

Insight idea:

- Which segment spends the most
- Which segment has longest stays
- Which segment is most loyal


`2. What tools did you use?`

- **Excel**: to clean data and calculate things like stay duration and revenue

- **Tableau**: to create dashboards and visualize trends 


`3. What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?`

- Longer lead time increases cancellation risk

- Certain market segments and deposit types strongly influence cancellations

- City Hotels have higher demand but also higher cancellations

- Peak booking months show clear seasonal demand patterns

`Solution`: Hotels can reduce cancellations by adjusting deposit policies, targeting loyal customers, and improving peak-season pricing strategies.




`4. How would a business or a community (for Social Impact Projects) benefit from your work?`

Hotels can use this analysis to:

- increase revenue by adjusting prices based on demand

- reduce losses from cancellations

- improve occupancy rates 

- understand customer behavior better 

- make smarter business decisions using data

Overall, it leads to higher profitability, smarter operations, and improved customer targeting.
